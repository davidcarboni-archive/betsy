Ford Mondeo Ghia X

Touch of luxury, solid runner

2.0 Petrol, 2002, 111K miles. This is a top spec model with a huge boot and a tow bar. She's a great runner: I've had the car a year, commuting from Cardiff to Newport, and it's never let me down. This is a solid vehicle with a touch of luxury and loads of space. Taken care of, it could last for years.

Full leather interior, cruise control, climate control, Alpine head unit, electric sunroof. The bobywork has some nicks and scratches, which is normal for a car of this age, and theres a bit of cosmetic damage around the rear, but she's a good looking car and very comfortable for long journeys.

If you google this model, you'll see information about a Duratec inlet manifold issue. This was taken care of by a previous owner.

Office for National Statistics

I bought this car in Spring 2015 for a project at the ONS and used it to get the team from Cardiff to Newport every day for a year so that we could do the job for Methods Digital. She's been solid and faithful and has easily managed transporting five guys up and down the A48.

This is the car that built the ONS website. The project was a big succeess in the statistics world and a huge leap forward for the ONS. Betsy the Teal Mobile was very much one of the team and a well loved character who supported us at every step along the journey.

Now the project's over I don't need so much space so it's time for Betsy to go and I'll be buying something a bit smaller, less practical and more fun: a Ford Focus CC
